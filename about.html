ABOUT US

We are street workout / calisthenics workout team founded in January 2017. Jan Simon the founder has been training this sport for over 4 years and wants to teach it to the younger generations so they won't end up with bad circle of people. In the past he has been training a lot of different sports like football, basketball, karate and kickbox. One day he was looking on youtube for some home strenght exercises and saw this guy called "Hannibal for king" doing gravity defying tricks and that is what motivated him to train. First he did basic strenght exercises like different kinds of pushups, pullups and dips. After few months of strenght exercises, switched to trick training like human flag, dragon flag, handstand, front lever and after that switched to some harder elements: planche, handstand pushups, weighted strenght exercises. He also joined competitions in Slovenia and was

  • 6th on his first freestyle competition
  • 2nd on his second freestyle competition
  • 3rd on his third freestyle competition
  • 1st in weighted pullups (+20 kg) category
He went from skinny 55kg to muscular with 75kg bodyweight and 180cm height with just calisthenics and dedication to become stronger than he was yesterday!
